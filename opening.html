Opening Growth Rank Complaints

Opening

Hypothesis

You might expect that for insurance compainies there would be a positive correlation between increases in customer satisfaction and increases in premiums written, better service trends in the same direction as more customers.

Data

The dataset being used is for New York state, for auto insurance and for the years 2009 through 2018.

To reduce pattern volatility, any small (i.e. less than $250M in Premiums Written in a year) companines were filtered out.

Tests

View the change in premiums written (see References for definition) of insurance compainies.

View the change in DFS rank (see References for definition) of insurance compainies.

View the change in complaints (see References for definition) of insurance compainies.

Test for positive correlation in the trend (UP or DOWN) of written premiums to the trend of DFS rank

Test for positive correlation in the trend (UP or DOWN) of written premiums to the trend of complaints

Results

For the selected dataset and methods of analysis, there was not an aggregate positive correlation found.
