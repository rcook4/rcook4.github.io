Opening Growth Rank Complaints

Opening

Hypothesis

There will be a strong positive correlation between customer satisfaction and premiums written for auto insurance compainies.

Data

The dataset being used is on auto insurance for New York state from 2009 to 2018.

To increase transparency of calculations using year and year previous the 2009 data was filtered out.

To reduce trend volatility all small (i.e. less than $250M in Premiums Written in a year) companines were filtered out.

Tests

View the change in premiums written (see References for definition) of insurance compainies.

View the change in DFS rank (see References for definition) of insurance compainies.

View the change in complaints (see References for definition) of insurance compainies.

Test for strong positive correlation in the trend (UP or DOWN) of written premiums to the trend of DFS rank

Test for strong positive correlation in the trend (UP or DOWN) of written premiums to the trend of complaints

Results

For the selected dataset and methods of analysis, there was not a strong positive correlation between customer satisfaction and premiums written for auto insurance compainies.
