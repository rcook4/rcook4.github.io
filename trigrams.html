Opening Trigrams Subjects Technologies Essay

This chart contains any trigram which was a top three usage for any given year.

It is striking that across 200 years only six different trigrams crack the top three.

This gives us an extremely high confidence that it was a balanced book selection across the years.

Had 1800s been about gold, 1900s about earthquakes, 2000s about Hollywood then maybe all the books were from California.

The y-axis is loosely based on the percentage of books the trigram appeared in that year.

The table is case-sensitive so aggregating all case spellings results in values higher than 1.

For example, imagine there is only ten books from 1800 and only one contains the word "history".

If the book has only the sentence "History of the world is written about more than history of the universe.".

Then the trigram "History of the" appears in ten percent of books and so does "history of the".

Which would aggregate to show in the chart as -history of the- with usage of 0.2.

This can safely be ignored as it is true of all trigrams and we are analyzing trends not seeking out a percentage.
